My Interests

Traveling

I love to travel. Especially when I can combine traveling with my other interests. Here are a few examples:

  • The Women of Winter ice hockey tournament in Rekjyavik, Iceland: I traveled with a group of like-minded women to explore Iceland and play ice hockey. It was amazing. Click here to read my friend's blog of the trip.
  • Friendsgiving in Baja, Mexico: Ate amazing food, hiked on a gorgeous mountain, stayed on a beatiful beach, and hung out with great friends. You can learn more about Friendsgiving travel by going to my friend's blog, Global At Heart.
  • Hockey in Hong Kong: I played in Asia's largest ice hockey tournament. The hockey was okay. The food was amazing. Hong Kong was awesome. I even got some hiking in.
  • Study abroad in Besançon, France: I spent six months traveling all over Europe, learning the French language and culture, eating (and learning how to cook) great food, and meeting interesting people.
  • Playing soccer in Scandanavia: In high school I played in the Pondus Cup in Copenhagen, Denmark and the Gothia Cup in Gottenberg, Sweden.

Backpacking

I love to go backpacking with my family. We've mainly hiked in northern Michigan and northern Ontario. Here are some of the places we've gone:

  • McCormick Tract in Michigan's Upper Penninsula
  • Pukaskwa National Park on the north shore of Lake Superior in Northern Ontario
  • Algonquin Provincial Park in Northern Ontario, just outside Toronto
  • Porcupine Mountains Wilderness State Park in Michigan's Upper Penninsula
  • Isle Royale National Park deep in the heart of Lake Superior
